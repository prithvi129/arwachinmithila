    About Us

    Arwachin means Exclusively New ! In Arwachin Mithila kala we nurture our cultural heritage of Mithila Painting in both contemporary and ethnic styles. For that we produce large numbers of new sub styles at its best. We represent the acclaimed beauty of Madhubani Painting on different surfaces like Apparels ( silk and cotton sarees, ladies suits, ladies Dupatta, t-shirt etc.) , Different sizes of Handmade Papers, wall hangings, ladies Purses, canvas and wall painting. Let's make life more colourful and impressive with Madhubani painting.
